About the Doctor

RD Dental Care Rangarajapram, situated in Chennai, India, specializes in dentistry and offers a range of treatments including Anterior & Posterior Root Canal Treatment, Composite Restoration, Consultation, Fixed Orthodontic Therapy (Ceramic), Flap Surgery (Per Quadrant), LASER Excision, Scaling, Temporary Dentures, and Surgical Wisdom Teeth Extraction. The clinic was established in 2007 and is led by skilled dentists Dr. Santhosh Kumar and Dr. Nandhini V, both specialized in Dentistry.
